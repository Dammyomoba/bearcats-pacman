How to Play

🎓
Your Goal: Collect all 4 academic milestones (grad cap, diploma, star, trophy) spread across the maze to earn your degree.
⌨️
Controls: Use Arrow Keys or WASD to move. Hold the key for continuous movement. Click the game area first if keys don't respond.
👻
Obstacles: Academic obstacles (Exam, Tuition Bill, Deadline, Conflict) hunt you from different corners. Avoid contact!
📚
Knowledge Dots: Small books scattered through the maze give +1 Knowledge each. Milestone goals give +5. Collect as many as you can!
💾
Data: Enter your First Name, Last Name, and M Number before starting. Your CSV is auto-downloaded when the trial ends.
⏱️
Time Limit: You have 60 seconds per trial. Complete all 4 milestones before time runs out to succeed!
